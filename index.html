Current Plastic Situation

How much plastic and waste do we produced?

In 1950 the world produced only 2 million tonnes per year.
By 2015, annual production had increased nearly 200-fold,
reaching 381 million tonnes.
In 2010, primary plastic production was 270 million tonnes,
yet plastic waste was 275 million tonnes.

How much oil do we use to make plastic?

Estimates vary by source, but tend to converge on a range
between 4 to 8 percent of global oil consumption.
